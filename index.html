How It Works

Four easy steps to complete safety

1

Register & Get Digital ID

Upon arrival at airport, hotel, or check-post, complete quick KYC verification and receive your blockchain-based digital tourist ID instantly.

2

Download Mobile App

Install Aegis Travel app, login with your Digital ID, set up emergency contacts, and optionally enable tracking features.

3

Travel with Confidence

AI monitors your journey 24/7, alerts you about geo-fence boundaries, provides safety scores, and offers local guidance.

4

Instant Emergency Response

In case of emergency, tap panic button for instant alert to nearest police, family, and authorities with your live location and digital ID.
